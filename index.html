What is SyluxV1?

SyluxV1 is a modular hacked client built for Minecraft Bedrock Edition. It’s designed for power users, testers, and creators who want total control.

Current Features

  • 🌓 Fulbright – See clearly in caves and at night
  • 🧱 Ore Outline – Highlights ores through walls
  • 🗡️ Reach – Hit enemies from farther away
  • ⛔ Disabler – Bypass server-side anti-cheats
  • 💨 Speed – Move faster than normal
  • 🪂 No Fall – Prevents fall damage
  • 🔍 ESP – See players, mobs, or items through walls
  • 📃 Custom GUI – Open menu with Right Shift
  • 🧩 Modular System – Enable/disable features easily
